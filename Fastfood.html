Positioning of Fast Food Elements

McDonald's is the largest fast-food chain in the world, serving over 69 million customers daily across more than 100 countries.

A Pizza Hut delivered a pizza to space in 2001, sending a vacuum-sealed pie to Russian cosmonauts aboard the International Space Station.

French fries are the most popular fast-food item, with billions of pounds consumed globally each year.

The first drive-thru restaurant was Red's Giant Hamburg in Springfield, Missouri, which opened in 1947.
